Festivals of India

Diwali

Diwali, the festival of lights, is one of the most popular Hindu festivals. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Holi

Holi, known as the festival of colors, is celebrated with vibrant colors and joy. It signifies the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil.

Durga Puja

Durga Puja is a major festival celebrated in West Bengal and other parts of India, honoring the goddess Durga's victory over the buffalo demon Mahishasura.
